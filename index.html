Who We Are

LoadYourTruck is a nationwide dispatching and logistics support company dedicated to keeping trucks loaded, profitable, and moving efficiently. We serve owner-operators, independent carriers, and fleets of all sizes by delivering professional dispatching, load coordination, and operational support. Our mission is simple: to maximize your earning potential while minimizing stress, downtime, and administrative burdens. We understand the unique challenges truckers face on the road — long hours, tight deadlines, unpredictable freight markets, and complex broker relationships. That’s why we step in as a trusted partner, handling the behind-the-scenes work so you can focus on driving and growing your business.

Our Story

LoadYourTruck was founded with a vision to modernize dispatching for today’s trucking professionals. What started as a small service helping local carriers quickly grew into a nationwide operation supporting multiple equipment types, lanes, and fleet sizes. Our experience in the logistics industry revealed what many carriers already knew: Dispatching can be overwhelming Many companies lack transparency Truckers often get undervalued in rate negotiations Paperwork and compliance slow down productivity We built LoadYourTruck to solve these challenges through professional service, technology, and a people-first approach.
